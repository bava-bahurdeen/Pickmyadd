Frequently Asked Questions.
  • How do I book Influencers for Promotion?
    • Register as a brand and complete your KYC process to access all Influencer pricing. Create your campaign, explore Influencers, add them to your wish list, and request their pricing. Once you receive the pricing details, you can easily book your chosen Influencer by making a secure payment through various methods (credit cards, UPI, NEFT, etc.). Collaboration begins once the payment is confirmed. Influencers receive payment from PickMyAd's escrow only after successful content publishing. For more information Read refund policy & cancellation policy here.
  • Why KYC verification & completion is needed?
    • KYC completion is absolutely necessary for any brand without which they can’t view any Influencer pricing for booking. This also helps Influencers to work with you more confidently.
  • What are documents needed for KYC completion?
    • Brands need to submit their company proof like Company cancelled Cheque / Bank Statement / Registration Certificate (anyone), Company Pan & Signatory Pan (both), and GSTIN certificate (if registered).
  • I have signed up on PickMyAd.com, what do I do next?
    • Complete your profile which helps brands in booking you. Then, Sit back and do your content creation, we will intimate you through email & Whatsapp in case of brand booking happens. Happy Influencing!
  • How much Will I be paid for my work?
    • It is completely free to sign up for both influencer and brands . PickMyAd will charge 10% from the brands upon successful completion of each campaign.
  • When Will I be paid for my work?
    • PickMyAd automatic system will compensate the amount you agreed with the brand within 24 hours of publishing the content and pasting the link in the particular booking inside the campaign dashboard.
  • Is there a way to delete my account in PickMyAd?
    • Oops, we’re sorry that you want to leave, but we respect your decision. sure in case you do want to delete you can delete your account form the setting sections of your PickMyAd account.
  • How to Join PickMyAd as an Influencer?
    • Sign up as an Influencer on www.pickmyad.com/signup.Go to "Brand Collab" module in the apps section and enable the module and Link your profile by including the URLs of your social media accounts, such as your YouTube channel and Instagram profile. Configure your account and let the brands easily book your services.
  • How does PickMyAd work for Influencers?
    • "PickMyAd collaborates with top-notch, KYC-verified brands. Only verified Brands can select you, request your pricing, and propose collaborations. You decide to accept or decline. Upon agreement, brands book your service in advance, empowering you to create compelling content. Once you publish the content, payment is securely transferred to you through PickMyAd's Escrow system."
  • Will PickMyAd have access to post on my Social Media Accounts?
    • Absolutely not. There's no need to link your account. Simply provide your social media profile URL to PickMyAd. Rest assured, we only access your public information. This process is highly secure and widely used by various organizations, ensuring your privacy and security remain intact at all times.
